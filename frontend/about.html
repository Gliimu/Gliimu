Our Story

Founded in the heart of Abuja, Gliimu Institute was born from a bold idea: what if education didn't just teach theory, but built creators? We saw a growing divide between what traditional classrooms offered and what the media industry actually needed. So we decided to do something about it.

Starting as a modest computer training center, we've since evolved into a full-fledged Institute of Media Technologies, shaping what we call Media Architects — professionals who don't just use tools but build the future of media, storytelling, and technology. Our mission remains clear: produce job-ready talents who can innovate, adapt, and lead.

What Guides Us

The core principles that define our culture, curriculum, and community.

Innovation First

We constantly update our tools, facilities, and curriculum to stay ahead of industry trends, ensuring our students are always learning the most relevant skills.

Integrity Always

Transparency is non-negotiable. From assessments to partnerships, we build trust by being honest, open, and fair with every student and stakeholder.

Community Over Competition

We believe real learning happens together. Our environment is built on collaboration, mentorship, and peer support — because we rise by lifting others.

Our Journey So Far

A timeline of growth, milestones, and the moments that shaped us.

2024

Gliimu began as a small creative training hub in Mpape, Abuja, with a handful of students and a big vision for hands-on computer skill training.

2025

We received official recognition from the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), solidifying our status as a legitimate educational institution committed to quality.

2026

We relocated to Gwarinpa, Abuja, to provide a more spacious, inspiring, and opportunity-rich environment for our growing community of learners.

2026

We launched our 24/7 Virtual Library, debuted our official website, and hosted the first-ever Gliimu Day — a celebration of creativity, learning, and community.
