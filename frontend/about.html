Our Story

Officially founded in the heart of Abuja, Gliimu Institute began with a simple observation: the gap between traditional education and industry needs was increasing. We set out to bridge that gap.

Our journey started as a small training center for computer training. Today, we have grown into an Institute of Media Technologies, offering diplomas in Full Stack Media Production or as we like to call it... (Media Architecture). Our mission remains the same: to prepare job-ready Media Architects.

Our Journey

Key dates in our journey of truly serving Africa.

2024

Founded as a small creative training hub in Mpape, Abuja with few students and a focus on computer skill training.

2025

Got licenced by the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) to legally operate as an educational institution.

2026

Moved to Gwarinpa, Abuja, for a better learning environment with better opportunities for our students.

2026

Hosted our Website and launched our 24/7 Virtual-Library & officially launched our first Gliimu Day.
